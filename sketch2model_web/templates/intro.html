Welcome to Sketch2Model

Sketch2Model is a geoscience hackathon project that uses open source image processing to quickly convert a whiteboard sketch into a color block model.

Try it out.

It works on all kinds of images

Sketch2Model works on all kinds of complex sketches. Try it out on your deepwater turbidite, mud volcano, or whatever. Sketch2Model uses some pretty cool image processing to recognize all the unique regions in your sketch and handles the borders.




Made to work with Modelr

Sketch2Model was developed to with modelr.io in mind. Take your completed model over to modelr and generate a forward seismic model in no time. Bring your geoscience collaboration sessions to the next level.

Make Sketch2Model work for you

Check out the Sketch2Model API and integrate Sketch2Model into your own application or workflow. Or just fork/contribute to the project on Github.
