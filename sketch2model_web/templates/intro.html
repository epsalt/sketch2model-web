Sketch2Model uses open source image processing to quickly convert a whiteboard sketch into a color block model.


Sketch2Model works on all kinds of cool and complex sketches. Try it out on your deepwater turbidite, mud volcano, or random doodle.


Then take your model over to modelr.io and convert it into a forward seismic model. Sketch2Model can help you take your geoscience brainstorming sessions to the next level.
