What is Sketch2Model?

Sketch2Model is a python based webapp that makes converting from schematic diagrams to color block models easy. Just take a photo of your drawing, upload, and let Sketch2Model do the rest!

This app is the continuation of a project from the 2015 Calgary Geoscience Hackathon organized by Agile Geoscience.

How does Sketch2Model work?

Sketch2Model uses the python library scikit-image to process and identify regions in the photos you upload. If you want to learn more or contribute check out Sketch2Model on GitHub.
